Vengeance Raids

What are Vengeance Raids?
When a TKO member is attacked—whether at the House of Pain or through a regular assault—we respond with a coordinated Vengeance Raid. The name of the enemy responsible will be posted here. Any member within battle range is encouraged to strike back and will be recognized for their bravery. A Hall of Fame will honor those who avenge their clanmates.

If you’ve been attacked:
If you’re targeted by an enemy and cannot post a guild message due to your rank, please contact GM Dealer in-game. The enemy’s name will be added to our list so others can assist in retaliation.

If you’ve avenged a clanmate:
If you defeat an enemy who attacked a TKO member, notify GM Dealer to be added to the Hall of Fame. If you can post guild messages, please share your victory with the clan. Admins and the Guild Master regularly review member stats and descriptions—if you mention your act of vengeance in your profile, it will be recognized and celebrated.
